Amount verification

Compares raw GEFS ensemble-mean precipitation, ANN-CSGD expected precipitation, and MRMS-GC observed precipitation in millimeters per 24 hours.

The fourth panel shows |GEFS − MRMS| − |ANN-CSGD − MRMS|. Positive values indicate that ANN-CSGD was closer to the observed amount.

Threshold verification

Compares raw GEFS member exceedance probability with ANN-CSGD exceedance probability.

MRMS-GC supplies the verifying precipitation amount, while the observed-exceedance contour identifies locations where the selected threshold occurred.
