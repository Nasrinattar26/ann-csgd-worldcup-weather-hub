Quick guide

How to read this dashboard

City forecast: Select a host city to view the chance of exceeding rainfall thresholds over time. Values are probabilities in percent.

ERO / Excessive Rainfall Outlook: The WPC ERO shows areas where rainfall may exceed flash-flood guidance within about 25 miles of a point. In this dashboard, ERO information is used as a reference for broader rainfall-risk comparison maps.

Forecast comparison maps: The CONUS ERO 2×2 maps are four-panel maps that compare ANN-CSGD rainfall-risk guidance with reference rainfall-risk/ERO information across the contiguous United States.

GRIB2 downloads: GRIB2 files are machine-readable gridded forecast data for technical users. They contain rainfall-risk probability fields that can be opened in meteorological or GIS software.

ERO definition adapted from the NOAA/NWS Weather Prediction Center Excessive Rainfall Outlook page.
